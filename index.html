Our Italian Creations

Baked fresh daily with love and tradition
Cannoli
Cannoli are classic Sicilian pastries with a crispy shell filled with sweet ricotta cream. Often topped with pistachios, chocolate chips, or candied fruit. They offer a perfect balance of crunch and creamy richness.
Sfogliatella
Sfogliatella is a traditional Neapolitan pastry known for its crisp, shell-like layers. It’s typically filled with a sweet semolina and ricotta mixture, flavored with citrus zest. Delicate, flaky, and rich—it's a true Italian bakery favorite.
Tiramisu
Tiramisu is a classic Italian dessert made with layers of coffee-soaked ladyfingers and mascarpone cream. It's topped with a dusting of cocoa powder for a rich, bittersweet finish. Light, creamy, and full of flavor—perfect for coffee lovers.
Biscotti
Biscotti are crunchy, twice-baked Italian cookies traditionally made with almonds. They’re perfect for dipping in coffee or sweet dessert wine. Crisp, nutty, and lightly sweet—a classic Italian treat.
Crostata
Crostata is a rustic Italian tart made with a buttery shortcrust pastry and filled with jam or fresh fruit. It’s often lattice-topped and baked until golden. Simple, fruity, and charming—perfect for any time of day.
Zeppole
Zeppole are light and airy Italian doughnuts, deep-fried until golden and dusted with powdered sugar. They can be filled with custard, ricotta, or jelly, especially during celebrations. Soft inside, crispy outside—a festive sweet delight.
